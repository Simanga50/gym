About IronForge

BUILT FOR
REAL RESULTS

IronForge has been Dublin's go-to strength and conditioning gym since 2014. Located in the heart of Thomas Street, we combine state-of-the-art equipment with expert coaching to help every member — beginner or elite — reach their goals.

We believe great fitness isn't about extremes. It's about consistency, community, and the right guidance. Our coaches build personalised programmes and our floor is always staffed so you're never training alone.
